Zack Rosen

Zack would be a Junior in computer science at the University of Illinois if he did not defer his enrollment to take a job on Howard Dean's presidential campaign as a web developer. Early this past summer he started an open source development project to create web-community software for Dean's grassroots campaign. Now in it's third month of development, DeanSpace is being adopted by campaign communities and state field offices to create peer-moderated websites that can share content and users accross a network of other campaign sites.
